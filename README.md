# SpatialLab — Where interaction, immersion and intelligence converge.

> Research & Development Lab at [CENFOTEC University](https://ucenfotec.ac.cr/), Costa Rica.
> Exploring new ways for people to interact with technology through Extended Reality (XR), Artificial Intelligence (AI), and emerging technologies.

---

## About

SpatialLab bridges immersive systems and human-centered design, translating technological innovation into real-world impact. We operate through interdisciplinary collaboration — computer science, psychology, design, and engineering — integrating academic research, experimental prototyping, and applied development.

## Mission

Push the limits of XR, AI, and interactive experiences — building solutions that are innovative, inclusive, and centered on human needs.

---

## Research Lines

### Interaction in Space
Investigating how XR and spatial computing transform physical environments into responsive, intelligent, and meaningful experiences.
- Immersive museums and cultural heritage
- Adaptive workplaces and interactive retail
- Digital-physical spatial overlays

### Education
Designing educational technologies that enhance engagement, comprehension, and experiential learning through immersive systems.
- Virtual STEM laboratories
- Immersive medical simulations
- AI-driven adaptive learning

### Accessibility
Building XR experiences that are inclusive and adaptive across different abilities and cognitive profiles.
- Real-time sign language in immersive environments
- Learning tools for neurodiverse audiences
- Cognitive accessibility in XR
